To investigate the structural basis of the observed entrenchment at sites 73 and 75, we analyzed crystal and cryo-EM structures of Fab fragments from the Protein Data Bank (PDB).
Structures were selected based on the following criteria: (1) human antibodies, (2) heavy chain V gene assigned to either IGHV1 or IGHV3 family, (3) unmutated germline residues at positions 71--75 (Chothia numbering), (4) presence of the entrenched amino acids at positions 73 and 75 (K73 and T75 for IGHV1; N73 and K75 for IGHV3), and (5) resolution $\leq$3.0~\AA.
V gene assignments were performed using partis \cite{Ralph2016}, and unmutated positions were identified by comparison to germline references from the AIRR Community OGRDB (\url{https://ogrdb.airr-community.org/germline_set/75}).
Four structures meeting these criteria were analyzed for each family: IGHV1 (PDB: 7X29, 2NY6, 6VY4, 8G3Z; resolution 2.0--2.8~\AA) and IGHV3 (PDB: 4H8W, 3BN9, 6ULE, 6PPG; resolution 1.85--2.75~\AA).

Structural analysis was performed using UCSF ChimeraX version 1.11.1 \cite{Pettersen2021}.
For each structure, we identified the heavy chain and mapped PDB residue numbers to the Chothia numbering scheme by locating the conserved sequence motif at positions 71--75.
PDB-to-Chothia offsets varied across structures (e.g., direct correspondence in 6ULE, 4H8W, and 3BN9; +1 offset in 8G3Z, 7X29, 6VY4, and 6PPG; +3001 offset in 2NY6), so residue mapping was verified individually for each structure by confirming the expected amino acid identities.
Hydrogen bonds involving Chothia sites 73--75 were identified using the \texttt{hbonds} command with default geometric criteria (0.4~\AA\ distance tolerance, 20.0$^\circ$ angle tolerance).
H-bonds were computed with \texttt{restrict any} to capture all interactions between sites 73--75 and any other residue in the structure, and water-mediated hydrogen bonds were recorded separately.
Only one copy of the heavy chain was analyzed per structure to avoid redundancy from crystallographic symmetry mates.
All residue numbering reported follows the Chothia scheme.
